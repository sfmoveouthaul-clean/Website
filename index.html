Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you move my belongings?

No. We are not a moving company. We clean apartments and remove unwanted items that you do not wish to keep.

How much does it cost?

Studios and one-bedrooms start at $199, two-bedrooms start at $299, and larger homes receive a custom quote.

What's included in the move-out cleaning?

We deep clean kitchens, bathrooms, floors, baseboards, surfaces, windowsills, and other high-touch areas to leave the home deposit-ready.

What can be hauled away?

We can remove furniture, appliances, trash bags, boxes, and general unwanted household items for responsible disposal or recycling.

How far in advance do I need to schedule?

We recommend reaching out as early as possible, but we can often accommodate shorter notice depending on availability.

Do you guarantee I'll get my security deposit back?

We cannot control landlord decisions, but we clean to commonly expected move-out standards to give you the best chance at a full return.

Are you insured?

Yes, we operate as a professional service and can provide insurance details during booking.
